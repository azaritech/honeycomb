    COMPASS_DIRECTION: string

    There's an (approximate) compass direction for each side of a hex. The right side of a pointy hex has the east ('E') compass direction. The bottom right side the southeast ('SE') direction, etc. This also means that pointy hexes don't have a north and south compass direction and flat hexes don't have a west and east compass direction.

    Number directions map to a side of a hex. A pointy hex's right side is 0, its bottom right side 1, its bottom left side 2, etc.

    public COMPASS_DIRECTION: string source

    There's an (approximate) compass direction for each side of a hex. The right side of a pointy hex has the east ('E') compass direction. The bottom right side the southeast ('SE') direction, etc. This also means that pointy hexes don't have a north and south compass direction and flat hexes don't have a west and east compass direction.

    Number directions map to a side of a hex. A pointy hex's right side is 0, its bottom right side 1, its bottom left side 2, etc. Number directions of flat hexes start at their bottom right side (0), their bottom side is 1, etc.
